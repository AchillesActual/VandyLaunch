Our Mission

VandyLaunch is dedicated to bridging the gap between innovative Vanderbilt startups and the resources they need to thrive. We believe that great ideas deserve the right support, and our platform makes it easier than ever for startups to connect with alumni investors, strategic advisors, and technical cofounders.

Our goal is to create a thriving ecosystem where Vanderbilt entrepreneurs can access capital, expertise, and talent through their university network. By facilitating these connections, we're building a foundation for long-term success and strengthening the Vanderbilt community.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the SAFE investment process work?

Our platform uses standardized SAFE (Simple Agreement for Future Equity) agreements. Startups can set their terms, and alumni can invest with as little as $1,000. We charge a 0.5% transaction fee on successful investments.

Who can be a strategic advisor?

Experienced Vanderbilt alumni can apply to become strategic advisors. We verify their expertise and match them with startups in relevant industries.

How do you match technical cofounders?

We use a combination of skills matching, industry experience, and mutual interests to connect founders with potential technical cofounders from the Vanderbilt community.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join VandyLaunch as a startup?

Create a profile through our platform and submit your startup information. Our team will review your application and get back to you within 48 hours.

What are the requirements for investors?

Investors must be verified Vanderbilt alumni or accredited investors. Complete our investor verification process to get started.

How do I schedule a demo?

Use our contact form above or email us directly at demos@vandylaunch.com to schedule a platform demonstration.

What support do you offer universities?

We provide full platform customization, technical support, and dedicated account management for university partners.
